Welcome to my site. Let me share some of my story.

Education

In 2014 I began studying at the University of Louisville.

I graduated three times:

  • 2018 - Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering - GPA 4.00
  • 2019 - Masters in Mechanical Engineering - GPA 4.00
  • 2020 - Masters in Business Administration - GPA 3.98

Work Experience

Since 2015 I have worked in various engineering and supervisory roles at Ford Motor Company. I am currently work as a Joining Engineer at the Kentucky Truck Plant in Louisville, KY.

Programming Experience

As part of my work at Ford, I have written applications in Python, VBA, and SQLite. I have also built independent websites using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Wordpress applications. On other projects, I have had experience with C++, Rockwell Automation RSLogix, and CAD programs.

Favorite Projects

Some of my favorite projects include:

  • A label generator application currently used to label all incoming inventory at the Ford Kentucky Truck Plant.
  • A capture-the-flag robot I made as an Engineering Masters project
  • A stock-picking web scraper I wrote during my MBA program

Other Interests

In my spare time, I am training for my black belt test in Niseido Jujitsu, a defensive martial art.

To unwind, I juggle balls, clubs, and knives or play the piano or guitar.
